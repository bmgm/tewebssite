About Us

Tejashree Electricals, founded in 2017, is a forward-thinking solar energy solutions provider and registered electrical contractor based in Maharashtra. As a start-up driven by innovation and integrity, we take pride in executing solar projects of all sizes with a commitment to safety, timely delivery, and quality. Our team blends technical expertise with a customer-centric approach to ensure every project meets high standards from day one. With strong relationships across the industry and an adaptive mindset, we continually improve by staying ahead of market trends and technology developments.
